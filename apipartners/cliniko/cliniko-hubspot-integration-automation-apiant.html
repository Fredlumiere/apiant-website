Key Features of Cliniko + HubSpot Integration

Automatically turn Cliniko data into actionable insights and automations within HubSpot, streamlining your operations and enhancing patient relationships.

Two-Way Patient Profile Sync

Automatically keep Cliniko patient profiles and HubSpot contacts perfectly synced in real-time. Demographics, preferences, appointment history, and 120+ key properties flow between platforms so your team always works with accurate data.

Next Appointment Sync

Always know when patients are scheduled or overdue for their next visit. Appointment details sync automatically into HubSpot, enabling reminders, no-show recovery, and gentle recall nudges for inactive patients.

Case Information Sync

Sync key case details into HubSpot including milestones, session limits, referral info, and financial data. Your team gets a complete lifecycle view to deliver personalized care and streamline operations.

Cliniko Appointments Pipeline

Track all appointments as deals in HubSpot, categorized by status -- Booked, Arrived, Did Not Arrive, or Cancelled. Automate follow-ups, assign deal values for revenue forecasting, and include telehealth URLs for virtual appointments.

Appointment Deal Amount

Automatically assign a value to each appointment deal based on billable items. Get clear insight into the financial value of every session, helping you forecast revenue and make smarter business decisions.

Cliniko Invoices Pipeline

Sync all invoices as deals in HubSpot for a clear view of your clinic's financials. Each deal includes status, dates, patient info, net amount, tax, discounts, and more -- enabling revenue forecasting and advertising ROI measurement.

Everything you need to know

Why is this API App better than DIY tools like Make, Appy Pie or Pipedream?
This API App is a turnkey, professionally-engineered integration solution built by experts with deep domain knowledge in both applications.

Unlike DIY tools, which leave you to build, debug, and manage integrations yourself, our integration is pre-built, thoroughly tested, and includes sophisticated logic that ensures data flows accurately, reliably, and on-time.

It’s configurable, so you can tailor how your apps communicate without coding. Plus, you get VIP customer support from specialists who understand your specific business needs. Within minutes, your systems are fully connected - no technical headaches, no trial-and-error.

Integrations aren’t side-projects; they’re critical software infrastructure. Trust them to professionals who've perfected it through years of experience.
How does the pricing compare to DIY integration tools like Make, Appy Pie or Pipedream?
It ultimately depends on how much data you’ll sync between your apps. DIY integration tools typically charge per "task" performed - these costs add up quickly, especially when your integration demands sophisticated logic, data transformations (like dates or currencies), caching, and complex workflows.

Our API Apps, however, are built with advanced features and integrations that simply can't be replicated using these DIY tools.

Price-wise, our solutions offer a clear advantage: at your selected usage level (e.g., number of clients), we often allow you to sync unlimited records, including client data, sales, transactions, and more. This makes our API Apps not only superior in capability but also far more cost-effective as your needs scale.
How does the two-way client sync work?
The sync from Cliniko → HubSpot is automatic. The sync from HubSpot → Cliniko is triggered by a custom property in HubSpot called "Push to Cliniko" which instantly pushes the contact to Cliniko as a patient or a contact (your choice). If a matching patient or contact already exists, an update is made. If not, a patient is created in Cliniko. The "Push to Cliniko" custom property can also be set by a form or a workflow in HubSpot.
Do I have to create the properties in HubSpot to receive the data from Cliniko?
You do not. Property creation in HubSpot is automatic upon install.
Are appointments, cases, invoices and payments two-way synced?
No. Appointments, cases, invoices and payments are only synced from Cliniko to HubSpot.
How much historical data is included with the integration?
One month of appointments and invoices are synced upon install. More historical transactions can be synced upon request for an additional cost.
Which HubSpot Hub do I need to run CRMConnect?
CRMConnect will work with the free version of HubSpot, but some features and benefits, such as Appointment and Sales pipeline require a HubSpot Pro Hub which also allows workflows. To set up even more powerful automations, a subscription to HubSpot’s Sales Hub Enterprise allows for auto-enrolling contacts in sequences. This is one of the most powerful features CRMConnect enables.
Are HubSpot workflows and sequences included?
No. CRMConnect only interprets and syncs data between Cliniko and HubSpot. This data then becomes very actionable, but it is up to you to build workflows, sequences, etc. If you are looking for help with this, please inquire within, as we can refer you to a HubSpot partner that is familiar with CRMConnect.
What happens to a Cliniko patient with no email?
A Cliniko patient with no email that’s synced with HubSpot will not have an email in HubSpot either.
How do I get advertising ROI reporting?
When you have HubSpot Marketing Hub at the enterprise level, you can go to Marketing / Ads, connect your ad accounts and configure your reports.
