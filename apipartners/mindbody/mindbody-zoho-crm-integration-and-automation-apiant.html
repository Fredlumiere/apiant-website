How does client syncing work between Mindbody and Zoho CRM?
Client syncing from Mindbody ‚Üí Zoho CRM happens automatically. Zoho CRM ‚Üí Mindbody sync is triggered by marking leads or contacts to sync manually or via automation.
What Mindbody data syncs into Zoho CRM custom objects?
Appointments, Client Services, Memberships, Contracts, Purchases, Purchased Items, and Payments each sync into dedicated custom objects within Zoho CRM.
Is this integration two-way for appointments and sales?
No. Appointment, membership, service, and payment data flow from Mindbody to Zoho CRM only. Client profiles have a two-way sync.
How often does data sync?
Most data‚Äîincluding appointments, memberships, and purchases‚Äîsync in real-time or within minutes. Comprehensive reconciliation occurs daily.
How much historical data is imported initially?
By default, appointments sync 7 days back and 30 days forward; sales and visits sync 1 month back. Additional historical data can be imported upon request.
What Zoho CRM edition is required?
CRMConnect requires Zoho CRM Enterprise or higher for optimal custom object management and advanced automation capabilities.
Are Zoho CRM workflows and automations included?
CRMConnect provides the synced data. You configure automations, workflows, and reports inside Zoho CRM. Assistance available via integration partners if needed.
What happens if a Mindbody client has no email?
A unique placeholder email is created in Zoho CRM, ensuring seamless syncing without interruptions.
How are duplicate emails handled?
Matching logic checks name and email; exact matches update existing contacts. Non-exact matches follow advanced matching rules detailed at info.apiant.com.
