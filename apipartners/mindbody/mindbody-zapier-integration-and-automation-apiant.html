Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

What is AppConnect, and how does it enhance my Mindbody experience?
AppConnect is an easy-to-use integration tool that seamlessly connects your Mindbody account with thousands of popular apps through Zapier - automating workflows, eliminating manual tasks, and boosting productivity without any coding.
Do I need technical skills to set up AppConnect?
No technical expertise is required - AppConnect is designed for easy setup, though basic familiarity with Zapier helps when connecting your favorite apps.
What are "payloads," and how do they affect my subscription?
A "payload" is a single event or data transfer between Mindbody and Zapier (like a new client or booking update). Your monthly subscription includes 1,000 payloads, with additional payloads available at $0.04 each.
What happens if I exceed my monthly payload limit?
When you reach your monthly limit of 1,000 payloads, any additional payloads are billed at $0.04 each. We'll notify you before you hit your limit, allowing you to manage usage or upgrade your plan if needed.
Can I use AppConnect with Zapier's free plan?
No, AppConnect requires a paid Zapier plan that supports webhooks.
Is there a trial period for AppConnect?
Yes, AppConnect comes with a 7-day free trial, giving you full access to all features and up to 1,000 payloads.
How can I get help with setup or troubleshooting?
Our friendly support team is available to help with setup or any questions. For complex integrations, you can also hire a Zapier Expert.
Can AppConnect handle multiple Mindbody locations or accounts?
Yes, AppConnect supports multiple Mindbody locations or accounts. For solutions tailored to 25+ locations, please contact our sales team.
How do I cancel or modify my subscription?
You can easily change or cancel your subscription anytime through your account settings. If canceled, your service continues until the end of the current billing period. Contact our support team if you need assistance.
