Everything you need to know

Why is this APIApp better than DIY tools like Make, Appy Pie or Pipedream?
This API App is a turnkey, professionally-engineered integration solution built by experts with deep domain knowledge in both applications.

Unlike DIY tools, which leave you to build, debug, and manage integrations yourself, our integration is pre-built, thoroughly tested, and includes sophisticated logic that ensures data flows accurately, reliably, and on-time.

Its configurable, so you can tailor how your apps communicate without coding. Plus, you get VIP customer support from specialists who understand your specific business needs. Within minutes, your systems are fully connected - no technical headaches, no trial-and-error.

Integrations arent side-projects; theyre critical software infrastructure. Trust them to professionals who've perfected it through years of experience.
How does the pricing compare to DIY integration tools like Make, Appy Pie or Pipedream?
It ultimately depends on how much data youll sync between your apps. DIY integration tools typically charge per "task" performed - these costs add up quickly, especially when your integration demands sophisticated logic, data transformations (like dates or currencies), caching, and complex workflows.

Our API Apps, however, are built with advanced features and integrations that simply can't be replicated using these DIY tools.

Price-wise, our solutions offer a clear advantage: at your selected usage level (e.g., number of clients), we often allow you to sync unlimited records, including client data, sales, transactions, and more. This makes our API Apps not only superior in capability but also far more cost-effective as your needs scale.
How does the two-way client sync work?
Mindbody ActiveCampaign syncs automatically. ActiveCampaign Mindbody happens when you add the Push to Mindbody tag (manually or via automation). If a match exists, the client is updated; if not, a new client is created in the chosen Mindbody site.
How often is data synced?
Profiles sync instantly. Bookings, visits, sales, memberships, and contracts post within seconds. Appointment and sales pipelines refresh every 15 min; daily visits reconcile once per day.
Are bookings, visits, sales, memberships, and contracts two-way?
No. Those activities flow only from Mindbody to ActiveCampaign. Two-way sync is limited to the client profile.
How much historical data is imported?
By default, appointments load 7 days back and 30 days forward; visits and sales load 1 month back. More history can be added for a small Mindbody API fee.
Which ActiveCampaign plan do I need?
Works on Lite, but deal pipelines and advanced automations need Plus or higher. SMS and attribution reports require Professional.
Are ActiveCampaign automations included?
CRMConnect only syncs data. You build the automations. Need help? We can refer you to an ActiveCampaign partner.
What happens if a Mindbody client has no email?
If a Mindbody client doesn't have an email, CRMConnect automatically creates a unique placeholder email using your configured domain, ensuring every client syncs seamlessly into ActiveCampaign.
